Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe is my family's homemade-style pepperoni pizza, passed down through generations. My parents shared it with me after learning it from my grandparents, making it a cherished family tradition. The pizza features a homemade tomato sauce, mouthwatering cheese sourced from a local farm, and uncured pepperoni for rich, authentic flavor. To give it a healthier twist, the dough is purchased from a local pizza shop that specializes in high-protein pizza dough, making each slice more filling without sacrificing taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of high-protien pizza dough
  • 1 cup homemade pizza sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese locally sourced
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 20-25 slices of uncured pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasonings
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes optional
  • Fresh Basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Prepare pizza sauce (by combining the tomato sauce, tomato paste, olive oil, minced garlic, oregano, basil, onion powder, garlic powder, sugar (if using), salt, and pepper in a small saucepan. Simmer over low heat for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.)
  3. Prepare the dough
  4. Brush the crust lightly with olive oil
  5. Spread the sauce
  6. Add the cheese
  7. Arrange the pepperoni
  8. Season the pizza
  9. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes
  10. Remove Pizza
  11. Garnish with fresh basil

    12. Family Tip(for the sauce)

    For the best flavor, let the homemade sauce simmer slowly, use freshly shredded cheese instead of pre-shredded cheese, and allow the pizza to rest for a few minutes before cutting. This helps the cheese set and makes each slice easier to serve.
