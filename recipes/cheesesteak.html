Cheesesteak Recipe

Description

This cheesesteak recipe has been a family favorite for years and is our go-to meal for gatherings and special occasions. We start with premium filet mignon from a local butcher, carefully shaving and finely chopping the meat by hand for the perfect texture. The steak is cooked on a hot grill with a touch of olive oil, creating a juicy, flavorful bite while keeping the meat tender. We top it with sautéed green peppers, caramelized onions, and a combination of melted American and Cooper Sharp cheese for the perfect balance of creaminess and flavor. To finish it off, we lightly brush the roll with garlic butter, adding the perfect final touch to an unforgettable homemade cheesesteak.

Ingredients

Cheesesteak

  • 2lbs filet mignon
  • Fresh hoagie rolls
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large green pepper
  • 1 large sweet onion
  • American cheese
  • Cooper sharp cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Garlic Butter

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley(optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder(optional, for extra flavor)

Optional Toppings

  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Banana peppers
  • Hot cherry peppers
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Prepare steak
  2. Prepare vegetables
  3. Make the garlic butter
  4. Heat the grill
  5. Cook the steak
  6. Combine the steak and vegetables
  7. Add Cheese
  8. Toast the rolls
  9. Assemble cheesesteaks
  10. Serve immediately
